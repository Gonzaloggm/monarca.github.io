La empresa

Cassandra castro

Cassandra is a certified psychiatric mental health nurse practitioner who provides integrative mental health services to patients across the lifespan. She specializes in the care of adolescents, Hispanic, and immigrant populations. She is well trained in the treatment of depression, anxiety, mood disorders, ADHD, and personality disorders. She firmly believes in whole person care, in listening to the specific needs of each individual to guide their treatment. 


Cassandra earned her MSN at Frontier Nursing University and has been serving the greater Seattle community since 2019. She is passionate about handmade tortillas, paragliding, and XC skiing.  

Nuestra historia

Cassandra ha vivido entre la Ciudad de Mexico y Seattle los últimos 4 años, estudiando y trabajando y disfrutando.

 Siempre ha sido su sueño servir a la gente de su comunidad en su propia clínica, de eso nacio Monarca Mental Health.
